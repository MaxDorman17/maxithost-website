Billing & Cancellations

How payments, cancellations, and refunds work for MaxitHost hosting services.

Last updated: January 2026

This page explains how billing works for MaxitHost hosting services, including payments, cancellations, refunds, and what happens if a payment fails.

Billing

  • Hosting is billed monthly in advance
  • Payments are processed securely by Stripe
  • Subscriptions renew automatically each month on your billing date
  • Invoices and receipts are sent automatically by Stripe to your email address

One-off Work & Extras

Work that falls outside your hosting package may be charged separately. This includes:

  • Website migrations from other hosts
  • Emergency fixes or urgent out-of-hours work
  • Custom development or configuration
  • Additional support beyond what's included in your plan

One-off charges may be invoiced separately or processed via Stripe. All extra work is discussed and agreed with you before any charges are made.

Cancellations

You may cancel your hosting at any time by contacting us via email.

  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
  • You will continue to have access until then
  • No partial refunds are given for unused time within a billing period

After cancellation:

  • Your hosting will be stopped at the end of the billing period
  • We can provide a copy of your website files upon request
  • Client data is removed after a short retention period (typically 30 days)

To cancel, please email help@maxithost.co.uk with your domain name or account details.

Refunds

  • Hosting fees are generally non-refundable once a billing period has started
  • Refunds may be considered at MaxitHost's discretion in exceptional circumstances
  • One-off work is non-refundable once the work has been started

If you believe you are entitled to a refund, please contact us to discuss your situation.

Failed Payments

If a payment fails:

  • Stripe will automatically retry the payment over the following days
  • You will receive email notifications from Stripe about the failed payment
  • Please update your payment method if needed via the link in the Stripe email

If payment is not resolved:

  • Your hosting may be suspended after reasonable notice
  • Continued non-payment may result in service termination
  • Off-boarding will follow our standard cancellation process

We will always try to contact you before suspending or terminating services due to payment issues.

Changes to Pricing

  • Prices may change over time as our costs and services evolve
  • You will be notified in advance of any changes to your hosting price
  • Price changes typically take effect from your next billing cycle after notice is given

Contact

For billing questions, payment issues, or to request a cancellation, please contact us:

Email: help@maxithost.co.uk
