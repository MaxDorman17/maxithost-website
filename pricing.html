Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "static website"?

A static website is built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It doesn't require a database or server-side processing. Most business brochure sites, portfolios, and landing pages are static.

Can you build my website for me?

We focus on hosting, not web design. However, we can recommend trusted designers if you need one.

What if I need to make changes to my site?

Email us the changes and we'll update your site. For frequent updates, we can discuss a workflow that suits you.

Do I keep ownership of my domain?

Yes. Your domain stays registered in your name, with your registrar. We never take ownership of customer domains.

What happens if I want to leave?

We'll provide all your website files so you can move to another host. No lock-in, no exit fees.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

Do you monitor uptime?

Yes. We run external, independent uptime checks and alerts.

Do you offer CI/CD?

Yes, as an optional add-on when it makes sense. Most static sites don't need it.

Need Something Different?

If your requirements don't fit these plans, get in touch. We're happy to discuss custom arrangements for larger projects.
