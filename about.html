Hosting Done Properly

MaxiHost is a UK-based managed hosting service built on the principle that hosting should be boring, secure, and reliable.

Our Approach

We believe good hosting shouldn't require your attention. You shouldn't have to think about SSL certificates, server updates, or security patches. That's our job.

Simplicity

One customer, one container, one site. No complicated dashboards or control panels. Just tell us what you need and we'll handle it.

Security

Every site is isolated in its own container. HTTPS is automatic. We monitor our servers continuously and keep everything updated.

Reliability

We use enterprise infrastructure hosted in secure European data centres. Our systems are monitored around the clock.

Honesty

We won't oversell you features you don't need. We won't promise things we can't deliver. If something isn't right for MaxiHost, we'll tell you.

Our Infrastructure

We don't cut corners:

  • Servers: Hetzner VPS infrastructure in Germany, known for reliability and performance
  • Isolation: Docker containers ensure each customer site is completely separate
  • Routing: Caddy reverse proxy with automatic HTTPS certificate management
  • Monitoring: Prometheus-based monitoring with alerts for issues before they affect you
  • Security: Server-to-server communication secured via WireGuard VPN

This isn't shared hosting where hundreds of sites compete for resources. Your site gets dedicated resources in an isolated environment.
