Domain Options

You have three choices for how visitors access your site:

Free MaxiHost Subdomain

Example: yourname.maxithost.co.uk

Ideal for personal projects or businesses without their own domain. HTTPS included automatically.

Included in all plans.

Your Own Domain (Recommended)

Example: yourbusiness.co.uk

Use your existing domain. Simply point your DNS to our server and we handle the rest. SSL certificates are configured automatically.

Most common for businesses.

Preview URL

Example: Temporary link during setup

During setup, we provide a temporary preview link so you can review your site before it goes live.

For testing only.

DNS Setup for Custom Domains

If you're using your own domain, you'll need to update two DNS records. That's it.

Record Type Host Points To
A @ Our server IP (we'll provide this)
A www Our server IP

No nameserver changes required. Once DNS is updated, we configure everything else automatically.

Your domain stays yours

  • We never take ownership of your domain
  • Your domain remains registered in your name
  • You can move it elsewhere at any time
  • SSL certificates are included at no extra cost
