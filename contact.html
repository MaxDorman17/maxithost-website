What to Include in Your Email

If you're enquiring about hosting

  • Your name and business name
  • Whether you have an existing website or need one built
  • Your domain name (if you have one)
  • Any specific requirements

If you're an existing customer requesting changes

  • Your website/domain name
  • A clear description of what you need changed
  • Any files, images, or text for the update

The more detail you provide, the faster we can help.

Response Times

We're based in the UK and generally available during extended business hours. Most emails receive a response within the same day.

For existing customers with urgent issues, please mark your email as urgent and we'll prioritise it.
